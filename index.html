About this system

Staff are organized by school section: Kindergarten, Grades 1–4, Grades 5–8, and Grades 9–12. Use Staff to manage the roster, Payroll to run pay by section or for everyone, and History for past runs. Figures use Ethiopian Birr (ETB). Records are saved in your browser on this device.
